how to play

Tap (or Space) to launch from your orbit. Time it so your direction aims at the next star. Release within one rotation for bonus points; chain fast captures to build a streak.

Dodge planets. Catch comets for bonus points. Binaries orbit a shared center — watch both stars. Black holes play like normal stars but bend space around them.
